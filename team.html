TEAM

Harsh Agarwal

Curator

Harsh is a high school graduate with IB Diploma. He writes the blog, “Running Thoughts”. He initiated and lead many projects while in high school with a passion to create a community in and around the city. He has held the position of President while in senior high school. Presently he is on a gap year because he wanted to explore himself better. “Taking a gap year in India has been such an adventure”, he said. His vision behind organising TEDxBhilwara is to engage like-minded people in local community and allow space to spark healthy conversations on ideas that matter.
